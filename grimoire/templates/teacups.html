Fortune Telling Teacups

Share on twitter or facebook

Fortune telling teacups are decorated with tasseomantic divination guides and imagery. They depict zodiac symbols, tea leaf patterns, or playing cards. This list is not comprehensive; The Mystic Tea Room has an excellent and more complete list, although without images.

Some of these cups depict racial stereotypes and slurs which were commonplace for their time, which we present here because they are, however regrettably, a part of history.
